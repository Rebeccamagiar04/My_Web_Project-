Our Role

Connecting deserving students with scholarship opportunities.

Our Mission

We believe where a student is born should never determine how far they can go.

Our mission is to bridge the information gap preventing refugee and underserved students from accessing higher education opportunities.

Through this platform, we simplify scholarship discovery, application guidance, university information and deadlines in one accessible place.

We also support the UNHCR 15by30 strategy by helping more students discover and apply for higher education.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every refugee student can easily access quality higher education opportunities, regardless of their background.

We aspire to become Africa's trusted scholarship platform connecting learners with universities, scholarships, mentorship and educational resources.

What We Do

  • Curate verified scholarship opportunities.
  • Share university admission information.
  • Explain application requirements.
  • Notify students about deadlines.
  • Provide scholarship application guidance.
  • Connect students with educational resources.

Our Goals

  • Help more refugee students discover scholarships.
  • Reduce the information gap in higher education.
  • Support the UNHCR 15by30 vision.
  • Encourage more students to apply successfully.
  • Build hope through accessible education.

Our Story

Living around Kakuma, we witnessed talented young people finish secondary school with dreams of attending university.

Too often, students missed life-changing opportunities—not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked information.

This platform was created to simplify scholarship searches and make higher education opportunities easier to find.

This is more than a website. It's a movement to make higher education possible for every deserving student.
